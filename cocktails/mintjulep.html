MINT JULEP

FLAVOR PROFILE

This ice cold refresher is made with whiskey as a base,
mixed with sugar syrup and muddled mint leaves. Completed by plenty of
crushed ice.

GOES WELL WITH

DEVILED EGGS, FRIED CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS

- ice
- 65 ml bourbon
- 10 mint leaves
- 12 ml sugar syrup

HOW TO MIX IT

STEP 1

Shake the bourbon, mint leaves and sugar syrup
with ice and strain into a highball glass filled
with crushed ice. Churn gently with a long-handled
spoon and top with more crushed ice.
Garnish and serve.

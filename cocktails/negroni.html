NEGRONI

FLAVOR PROFILE

This apertif is made with equal parts gin, Red Vermouth and Campari.
The red drink is garnished with orange for an extra kick of flavour.

GOES WELL WITH

BACON WRAPPED DATES, BRUSCHETTA

INGREDIENTS

- ice
- 25 ml gin
- 25 ml Red Vermouth
- 25 ml Campari
- orange slice

HOW TO MIX IT

STEP 1

Pour the gin, vermouth and Campari into a mixing glass
or jug with ice. Stir well until the outside of the
glass feels cold.

STEP 2

Strain into a tumbler and add 1 large ice sphere or some
fresh ice, and add in an orange slice
